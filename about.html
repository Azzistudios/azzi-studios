The Studio

Azzi Studios is a creative studio based in Beirut. We produce campaigns and visual content for luxury, beauty, and lifestyle brands — combining creative direction, set design, and full production from brief to final image.

Our founder trained as an architect. It shows in the work.

The Work

We read every brief twice. Once for what it says, once for what it doesn’t.

The brands that work with us want more than a campaign. They want a visual identity that holds — one that looks considered, not produced.

We bring the direction, the set, and the team. Every decision is made before the camera arrives.

We favour long-term collaborations over one-off projects.
