Services  ·  Service 01

Campaign Production in Beirut

We produce campaigns end to end from our studio in Beirut — one team carrying a brand from the first brief to the final, delivered image. Creative direction, set, crew and post, under one roof.

What’s included

Creative Direction · Art Direction · Photography · Videography · Set Design · 360 Campaigns · Model Shoots · Music Videos

Approach

Every campaign starts with a conversation, not a shot list. We interrogate the brief — what the brand is really trying to say — then build the creative direction, the set and the team around that single idea.

Because we own the whole pipeline — direction, photography, video, set design and production — nothing gets lost in handoffs between agencies. The look you approve in pre-production is the look that lands in the final delivery.

We work with fashion, beauty, lifestyle and hospitality brands across Lebanon and the wider region, scaling from a focused product launch to a full 360 campaign with stills, motion and a music video.

Who it’s for

For brands in Beirut and across the GCC and MENA region who need a campaign that looks exactly the way they imagined — and a single studio accountable for getting it there.
