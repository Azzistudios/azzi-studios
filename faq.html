Questions.

The things brands ask us most — where we work, what we do, and how a project comes together.

01 Where is Azzi Studios based, and do you work internationally? +

Azzi Studios is a creative studio based in Beirut, Lebanon. We produce work across the region and travel internationally for campaigns, shoots and installations.

02 What services does Azzi Studios offer? +

Five. Campaign Production — creative direction, art direction, photography, videography and set design. Visual Content — product photography, lookbooks, reels and monthly content. VM & Window Display — window display, visual merchandising, retail environments and pop-ups. Visual Identity — logo, typography, colour systems, brand guidelines and packaging. Social Media Management — content strategy, calendars, community management, account growth and paid social.

03 What kinds of brands do you work with? +

We work with fashion, beauty, lifestyle and hospitality brands — from established names to new labels launching their first campaign.

04 What does a typical project look like? +

Every project starts with a brief and a conversation. We handle creative direction, set, team and production from end to end, so you get one studio with full creative ownership from concept to final delivery.

05 Do you offer monthly or ongoing content production? +

Yes. Alongside one-off campaigns we produce monthly visual content on retainer — product photography, reels and social-ready assets — for brands that need a consistent stream of imagery.

06 How do we start a project with Azzi Studios? +

Email hello@azzistudios.com or message us on WhatsApp with a few words about what you are working on. We favour long-term collaborations and tailor-made approaches.
