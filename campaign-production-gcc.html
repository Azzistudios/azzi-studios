Campaign Production Across the GCC

We're a creative studio based in Beirut, producing campaigns across the GCC — Dubai, Riyadh, Jeddah and the wider Gulf. A regional eye, full production, and one team accountable from brief to final image.

What we produce

Creative Direction · Art Direction · Photography · Videography · Set Design · 360 Campaigns · Visual Content

Approach

Beirut has long been the creative engine of the region — a deep bench of directors, photographers, stylists and set builders. We bring that talent to brands across the Gulf, travelling for shoots and installations or producing out of our studio and delivering region-wide.

We handle the whole pipeline — creative direction, photography, video, set design and post — so a brand in Dubai or Riyadh gets a single studio accountable for the campaign, not a chain of agencies and freelancers.

We work with fashion, beauty and lifestyle brands that want a campaign with a considered, editorial sensibility — scaled from a focused launch to a full 360 rollout.

Who it's for

For fashion, beauty and lifestyle brands in Dubai, Riyadh, Jeddah and across the GCC that want a campaign produced end to end — from Beirut, across the region.
